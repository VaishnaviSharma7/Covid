What Are The Main Symptoms?

COVID-19's telltale signs: fever, cough, and shortness of breath. Knowing them is our armor, swiftly identifying potential threats and fortifying our collective defense against the unseen adversary.

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches And Pains
  • Shortness Of Breath
